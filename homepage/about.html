About Muzz's Book Collection

Muzz's Book Collection is a bookstore owned by Muzamil Bin Tasleem Ahmad, a passionate book lover and a student at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg where he is currently studying International Information Systems.

Founded in 2019, Muzamil's vision was to provide a wide variety of books for readers in different languages, ranging from Urdu to English and Persian. Over the years, the bookstore has grown significantly, and Muzamil now proudly owns three locations and has expanded his business to include a cozy book reading cafe, where readers can relax with a book and a cup of coffee.

His passion for books and reading drives him to continuously offer an exceptional selection of literary works to all book enthusiasts, young and old alike. Whether you're looking for your next great read or just want to enjoy the atmosphere, Muzz's Book Collection is the place for you!
