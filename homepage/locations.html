Our Locations

We are proud to have three locations in different cities, offering our customers a chance to experience our books and services in various places. Here’s where you can find us:

1. DHA, Lahore, Pakistan

Our first location is in the upscale area of DHA Lahore, offering a wide selection of books in a serene environment perfect for book lovers.

Address: Muzz's Book Collection, Sector Z, DHA Lahore, Pakistan.

2. Bahria Town, Karachi, Pakistan

Our second location is in Bahria Town Karachi, a growing community with a love for books. Visit us for an extensive collection of books and more!

Address: Muzz's Book Collection, Bahria Town, Karachi, Pakistan.

3. Schwiegerstraße, Nuremberg, Germany

Our third and international location is in Nuremberg, Germany. We bring our love for books to the heart of Europe, offering the best collection of English, Urdu, and Persian literature.

Address: Muzz's Book Collection, Schwiegerstraße, Nuremberg, Germany.
